Dr. Ashok Kumar Mittal

Founder Chancellor of Lovely Professional University (LPU) & Member of Parliament (Rajya Sabha)

A law graduate from GNDU Amritsar, Dr. Mittal transformed his family's confectionery business into India's largest single-campus private university. Under his visionary leadership, LPU has earned NAAC A++ grade and ranks 27th in NIRF Overall Category. He is a strong advocate for making education industry-relevant and accessible to all.
